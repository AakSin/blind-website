About Us

We're changing the way college students make new friends and find love.

We're giving them control of their social life and mental health.

Meeting new people and making new friends you vibe with is hard. Finding love is harder. This is why more than 1 in 4 college students feel lonely often or always. This leads to bad mental health and overall has a negative impact on an individual's academic, social and professional life.

That's why we created Blind, a social life app that helps users meet new people outside their friend circle and find love, both in a more meaningful and exciting way through blind matching. Blind helps users create a healthy social life consisting of good friends they vibe with and romantic relationships where they can be their true self and not pretend to be someone else, creating a reliable support system instead of struggling to make new friends or meet new partners. It’d significantly create good mental health by cutting down loneliness and creating a thriving social life that will further improve their overall college life.

Don't compare us to Tinder or Bumble. The apps on the market focus on swiping left or right on people within seconds based on their looks, promoting superficiality. They do not consider a user’s personality and vibe, becoming used for hookups rather than socializing to create meaningful friendships or love. Also, the focus on appearance leads to unhealthy ‘beauty’ standards that users try to achieve, instead of bringing out and becoming their true selves. Blind is different, wholesome and better.
